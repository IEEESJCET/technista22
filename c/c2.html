CIRQTER

IEEE SB SJCET brings before you CIRQTER - an online competition to test your knowledge on electronic circuits.

RULES AND REGULATIONS
  • 1. The contestant should be aware of
    RLC Circuit
    Circuit analysis in the S-Domain [z(s) and y(s)]
    Two port networks
    Basic Nodal & Mesh analysis

  • 2. Participant can answer the required questions in an A4 sheet and upload it to the google form, which will be provided to you via email.

  • 3. The best circuiter will be awarded with cash prize and certificates.

  • 4. All the participants will be provided with certificates and excellence ceritificates too for the good analyser.

  • 5. You should submit your answers within the respective time as mentioned.
