HACK FOR LIFE

IEEE SB SJCET as part of TECHNISTA'20 , an online tech fest, brings before you an online ideathon to fire up your creative nerve.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

1. Participants are to solve the problems in the sight of;

  • Entrepreneurs.
  • Households.
  • Law Enforcements officers.
  • Healthcare.
  • Education
  • Natonal and International affairs.


  • 2. The ideathon is an individual event.

  • 3. Your solutions must be relevant to this pandemic that the world is going through.

  • 4. Participants are provided with 5 days to submit their ideas.

  • 5. Attach use case diagrams, dependencies, and total expense along with your idea in a doc file and attach the same in the google form that we will be providing you via email

  • 6. The abstract should be a minimum of 200 words and a maximum of 500 words

  • 7. The judging criteria depends on Practical and Social implementation of the technology along with its social impact.Come up with suitable,benevolent and warmhearted solutions.

  • 8. Entries are restricted to one entry per participant.
